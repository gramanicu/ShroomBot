ShroomBot

Hello there !

ShroomBot is a Discord Bot that can show League of Legends player's stats, the weekly rotation, and a few other things.

Example commands

  • -help - available commands
  • -getinfo $(summoner) $(region) - summoner information
  • -rotation - the weekly rotation
  • -quote - a random Teemo quote

Even more commands could be implemented in the future
